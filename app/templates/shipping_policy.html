Shipping Policy

Our Shipping Methods

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs:

  • Standard Shipping (5-7 business days)
    • Free for orders over $50
    • $5.99 for orders under $50
  • Express Shipping (2-3 business days)
    • $12.99 flat rate
    • Free for orders over $100
  • Next Day Delivery
    • $24.99 flat rate
    • Order must be placed before 2 PM EST
Processing Time
  • Orders are processed within 24 hours Monday-Friday
  • Orders placed after 2 PM EST will be processed the next business day
  • Orders placed on weekends will be processed on Monday
International Shipping: Currently, we only ship within the United States and Canada. For Canadian orders, additional customs fees may apply.
Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships:

  • You'll receive a shipping confirmation email
  • Tracking number will be provided
  • Track your package through our website or carrier's site
  • Email updates at key shipping milestones
